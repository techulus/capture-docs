## Setup

The Capture WordPress plugin makes it easy to integrate screenshot and PDF capture capabilities directly into your WordPress site. With the plugin, you can:
- Capture screenshots and PDFs from your WordPress dashboard.
- Embed automated screenshots in your posts and pages.
- Use shortcodes to display dynamic content captures.

To get started, visit our [WordPress plugin](https://capture.page/wordpress-plugin) page for installation instructions and usage examples.
